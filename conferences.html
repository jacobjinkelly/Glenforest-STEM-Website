Canadian Youth STEM Conference 2017

May 25, 2017 | Pearson Convention Center

Glenforest STEM hosted a variety of inspirational speakers from the STEM fields to inspire and motivate delegates into pursuing STEM professions. Speakers included David Miller, CEO of World Wildlife Fund, Elizabeth Dowdeswell, Lieutenant Governor of Ontario, Chris Fonseca, Mississauga City Councillor, and Peter Fonseca, Member of Parliament.

A set of hands-on activities hosted by Ontario universities and industry leading companies allowed participants to develop cooperative skills in creating innovative solutions.

A short one on one mentoring session in which students were given the opportunity to ask questions and interact with a range of highly qualified STEM field professionals.

Xperience STEM Conference 2015

In November 2015, we hosted our first ever conference, named the Xperience STEM Conference 2015.

We hosted 50 speakers and 20 workshops for over 1800 delegates, showcasing them the various careers in the STEM Fields.

Our Xperience STEM Conference was Canada's largest student-run conference, a huge success. We thank our sponsors and supporters for the wonderful opportunity.
